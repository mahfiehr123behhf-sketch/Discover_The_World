Bangkok

Thailand

★★★★★ 4.6 Rating

Overview

Bangkok is a dynamic city where ancient temples stand alongside modern skyscrapers. Known for its ornate shrines, vibrant street life, and bustling markets, the city offers an intoxicating mix of tradition and modernity. From floating markets to luxury malls, from street food to fine dining, Bangkok is a sensory feast.

Best places to visit

Wat Pho

Home to the famous Reclining Buddha, one of Bangkok's oldest and largest temples.

Grand Palace

The former royal residence with stunning architecture and the sacred Emerald Buddha.

Chatuchak Market

One of the world's largest weekend markets with thousands of vendors selling everything imaginable.

City insights

Climate

Tropical climate with hot, humid weather year-round. The cool season (November-February) is the most comfortable time to visit.

Culture

Deep Buddhist traditions, royal heritage, and a warm, welcoming culture known for its hospitality and respect for tradition.

Cuisine

World-famous street food scene with pad Thai, tom yum, and countless other dishes that are both flavorful and affordable.
