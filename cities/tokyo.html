Tokyo

Japan

★★★★★ 4.8 Rating

Overview

Tokyo blends luminous futurism with deep-rooted Japanese tradition. Neon districts pulse with energy while tranquil shrines hide in side streets, offering an ever-changing cityscape that rewards both curiosity and calm.

Best places to visit

Shibuya Crossing

The world-famous scramble that captures Tokyo's non-stop pulse.

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple, nestled within the historic Asakusa area.

Tokyo Skytree

A soaring tower with dazzling city views, especially at dusk.

City insights

Climate

Four distinct seasons, highlighted by cherry blossoms each spring.

Culture

A mix of pop-culture icons, anime, and time-honored tea ceremonies.

Cuisine

Home to sushi, ramen, and late-night street bites like takoyaki and yakitori.
