Lisbon

Portugal

★★★★★ 4.7 Rating

Overview

Lisbon is a charming coastal capital built on seven hills, known for its colorful tiled buildings, historic trams, and stunning viewpoints. The city combines old-world charm with modern innovation, offering everything from ancient castles to contemporary art galleries. Its warm climate, friendly locals, and vibrant culture make it a delightful destination.

Best places to visit

Belém Tower

A 16th-century fortified tower, a symbol of Portugal's Age of Discovery and UNESCO World Heritage site.

Jerónimos Monastery

A magnificent Manueline-style monastery showcasing Portugal's maritime heritage and architectural grandeur.

Alfama District

The oldest neighborhood with narrow streets, Fado music, and stunning views from its hilltop location.

City insights

Climate

Mediterranean climate with mild winters and warm summers. The city enjoys over 300 days of sunshine per year.

Culture

Rich maritime history, Fado music tradition, vibrant street art, and a laid-back lifestyle that values good food and conversation.

Cuisine

Famous for pastéis de nata, fresh seafood, grilled sardines, and excellent Portuguese wines at affordable prices.
