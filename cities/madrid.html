Madrid

Spain

★★★★★ 4.7 Rating

Overview

Madrid is Spain's vibrant capital, known for its elegant boulevards, world-class art museums, and lively nightlife. The city combines royal history with contemporary culture, offering everything from historic palaces to cutting-edge galleries. Its warm, welcoming atmosphere and late-night dining culture make it a captivating destination.

Best places to visit

Prado Museum

One of the world's greatest art museums, housing masterpieces by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.

Royal Palace

The official residence of the Spanish royal family, showcasing opulent rooms and art collections.

Retiro Park

A beautiful 350-acre park perfect for strolling, boating, and escaping the urban bustle.

City insights

Climate

Continental climate with hot summers and cold winters. Spring and autumn offer the most pleasant weather for exploration.

Culture

Rich cultural scene with flamenco, world-class museums, vibrant festivals, and a passionate love for football and the arts.

Cuisine

Famous for tapas, jamón ibérico, and traditional Spanish dishes. The city's food scene ranges from casual tapas bars to Michelin-starred restaurants.
