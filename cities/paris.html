Paris

France

★★★★★ 4.9 Rating

Overview

Paris, the City of Light, is a poetic mix of romance, history, and art. Elegant boulevards line the Seine, legendary museums guard masterpieces, and sidewalk cafés invite hours of people watching. From the Eiffel Tower to Montmartre, every neighborhood feels like a postcard.

Best places to visit

Eiffel Tower

The symbol of Paris with sweeping panoramas from day to night.

Louvre Museum

A world-class museum housing the Mona Lisa and countless treasures.

Montmartre

A bohemian hilltop district crowned by Sacré-Cœur and artist studios.

City insights

Climate

Mild weather with crisp winters and pleasant springs; April–June and September–October are ideal.

Culture

The capital of fashion and fine arts with galleries, couture, and open-air markets.

Cuisine

Indulge in buttery pastries, artful desserts, and refined bistro classics.
