Rio de Janeiro

Brazil

★★★★★ 4.8 Rating

Overview

Rio de Janeiro is a seaside city bursting with energy. Golden beaches, dramatic mountains, and samba rhythms create an atmosphere that feels like a celebration every day.

Best places to visit

Christ the Redeemer

An iconic statue atop Corcovado Mountain with sweeping views of Guanabara Bay.

Copacabana Beach

A lively shoreline perfect for swimming, strolling, and soaking up carioca vibes.

Sugarloaf Mountain

Ride the cable car to panoramic vistas of the city, ocean, and islands.

City insights

Climate

Tropical warmth year-round with hot, humid summers and breezy winters.

Culture

Defined by carnival parades, samba rhythms, and vibrant street art.

Cuisine

Try feijoada, fresh tropical juices, and coconut-infused desserts.
