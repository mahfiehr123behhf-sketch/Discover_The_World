Amsterdam

Netherlands

★★★★★ 4.7 Rating

Overview

Amsterdam is a charming city of canals, historic architecture, and world-class museums. Known for its artistic heritage, elaborate canal system, and narrow houses with gabled facades, the city offers a unique blend of history, culture, and modern lifestyle. Bicycles are the preferred mode of transport, adding to its distinctive character.

Best places to visit

Rijksmuseum

The national museum housing masterpieces by Rembrandt, Vermeer, and other Dutch masters.

Anne Frank House

A moving museum in the hiding place where Anne Frank wrote her famous diary during WWII.

Canal Ring

UNESCO World Heritage site with beautiful 17th-century canals perfect for boat tours.

City insights

Climate

Maritime climate with mild temperatures year-round. Spring brings beautiful tulip blooms, while summer offers pleasant weather for canal cruises.

Culture

Rich artistic heritage, progressive values, and a vibrant cultural scene with festivals, music, and world-renowned museums.

Cuisine

Traditional Dutch treats like stroopwafels and cheese, plus diverse international cuisine reflecting the city's multicultural population.
