Rome

Italy

★★★★★ 4.8 Rating

Overview

Rome, the Eternal City, pairs ancient grandeur with modern Italian life. Historic piazzas, ornate fountains, and timeless ruins sit beside bustling trattorias and designer boutiques.

Best places to visit

Colosseum

An ancient amphitheater telling stories of gladiators and empire.

Vatican City

St. Peter’s Basilica, the Vatican Museums, and Michelangelo’s art.

Trevi Fountain

Toss a coin and make a wish at Rome’s most beloved fountain.

City insights

Climate

Mediterranean weather with warm summers and mild winters.

Culture

Museums, contemporary art, and lively street life in classic piazzas.

Cuisine

Famous for pizza, pasta, and gelato served in cozy trattorias.
