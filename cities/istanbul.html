Istanbul

Turkey

★★★★★ 4.8 Rating

Overview

Istanbul is a mesmerizing city that bridges Europe and Asia, where ancient history meets modern vibrancy. Once the capital of the Byzantine and Ottoman empires, the city is home to stunning mosques, grand palaces, and bustling bazaars. The Bosphorus Strait divides the city, creating a unique cultural and geographical fusion.

Best places to visit

Hagia Sophia

A magnificent architectural marvel that has served as both a cathedral and mosque throughout history.

Blue Mosque

An iconic mosque with six minarets and stunning blue tile interiors, a masterpiece of Ottoman architecture.

Grand Bazaar

One of the world's oldest and largest covered markets with over 4,000 shops selling everything from spices to jewelry.

City insights

Climate

Temperate climate with hot summers and mild winters. Spring and autumn offer the most pleasant weather for exploring the city.

Culture

A rich blend of Eastern and Western influences, with deep historical roots, vibrant markets, and a thriving contemporary arts scene.

Cuisine

World-renowned Turkish cuisine featuring kebabs, mezes, baklava, and Turkish delight, with influences from both continents.
