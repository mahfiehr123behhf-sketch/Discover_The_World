Cairo

Egypt

★★★★★ 4.7 Rating

Overview

Cairo is a bustling metropolis where ancient Egypt meets vibrant modern life. The pyramids and Sphinx guard the desert horizon while downtown streets, markets, and cafés pulse with color and music.

Best places to visit

Giza Pyramids

One of the world’s ancient wonders and Egypt’s enduring icon.

Egyptian Museum

Home to thousands of priceless artifacts from every pharaonic era.

Khan el-Khalili

A historic souq brimming with crafts, spices, and lively cafés.

City insights

Climate

Desert climate with hot summers and mild winters; October–April is most comfortable.

Culture

A cultural capital with theater, classic Arabic music, and literary cafés.

Cuisine

Savor koshari, falafel, ful, and sweet treats like basbousa and umm ali.
