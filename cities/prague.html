Prague

Czech Republic

★★★★★ 4.8 Rating

Overview

Prague, the "City of a Hundred Spires," is a fairy-tale destination with stunning Gothic and Baroque architecture. The historic center is a UNESCO World Heritage site, featuring cobblestone streets, medieval bridges, and grand castles. The city's rich history, vibrant culture, and affordable charm make it one of Europe's most beloved destinations.

Best places to visit

Prague Castle

The largest ancient castle complex in the world, offering stunning views and centuries of history.

Charles Bridge

A historic stone bridge adorned with statues, connecting the Old Town with the Lesser Quarter.

Old Town Square

The heart of Prague with the Astronomical Clock, colorful buildings, and vibrant atmosphere.

City insights

Climate

Continental climate with warm summers and cold winters. Spring and early autumn provide ideal weather for exploring the city on foot.

Culture

Rich cultural heritage with classical music, literature, and arts. The city has a vibrant nightlife and thriving café culture.

Cuisine

Hearty Czech cuisine featuring goulash, dumplings, and roast pork, complemented by world-famous Czech beer.
